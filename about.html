About Faculty of Science Education

The Faculty of Science Educaton is committed to training future educators in science-related disciplines. The faculty combines theoretical knowledge with practical teaching skills to produce competent graduates.

To produce competent science educators equipped with modern teaching skills.

To provide quality education, research and innovation in science education.

VISION

"To be the best specialised University of Educaton in Africa with a niche in the: integration of teaching and learning; advancement of knowledge through research and scholarship; leadership in service and development; and in producing teachers and manpower suited to the challenges of the contemporary global knowledge economy"

MISSION

"To be an incubator for the training and production of highly qualified, top-notched and globally relevant professionals who will serve as teachers, administrators, supervisors, counselors and other essential manpower in the field of education, community service, industry and development at the national and international levels"
